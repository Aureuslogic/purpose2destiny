About Us

Purpose2Destiny exists to help you create space — to pause, reflect, heal, and rediscover who you are now.

We help accomplished men and women aged 45 to 75 who feel stuck in an identity that no longer fits them to rediscover who they are, recognise where they still matter, heal what is holding them back, and activate their purpose so they can confidently step into a meaningful next chapter where age becomes an advantage, not a limitation.

Taiwo and Kehinde Adesina

Twin sisters and the founders of Purpose2Destiny, a clarity-led coaching and guidance practice that supports adults aged 45 to 75 who are navigating identity shifts, life transitions, and questions of purpose, relevance, usefulness and direction.

Their clients are often thoughtful, capable and accomplished individuals who have spent many years meeting responsibilities, supporting others and building careers, families and communities.
