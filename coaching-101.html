Coaching 101

What is coaching, and how does it actually work?

If you have never worked with a coach before, these are the questions most people ask first.

What is coaching?

Coaching is a collaborative process that helps people gain clarity, identify goals, overcome challenges, and move forward with confidence.

What does "clarity led coaching" mean?

Clarity led coaching focuses on helping people understand themselves more deeply before making major life decisions. Instead of rushing people into action, we help clients reflect honestly, identify what feels misaligned, understand what matters now, reconnect with their values, strengths, and inner truth, and make decisions from calm awareness rather than fear or urgency.

How is coaching different from counselling?

Counselling often focuses on understanding and working through past experiences and emotional difficulties. Coaching focuses on helping people move forward from where they are now toward where they want to be. Our work may include reflection on past experiences, but our focus is helping clients create positive change moving forward.

How is coaching different from therapy?

Therapy is designed to diagnose and treat mental health conditions. Coaching focuses on personal growth, clarity, self-awareness, decision making, confidence, purpose, and future direction.

How does coaching with Purpose2Destiny work?

Our coaching process is reflective, structured, and conversational. We guide clients through honest reflection, identity building exercises, clarity building exercises, emotional awareness, purpose and alignment work, gentle action planning, and confidence rebuilding. Sessions are calm, supportive, and practical.
