Purpose2Destiny FAQ

About Purpose2Destiny

What is Purpose2Destiny?

Purpose2Destiny is a clarity led coaching and guidance practice founded by twin sisters Taiwo and Kehinde Adesina. We help thoughtful, capable adults aged 45 to 75 who are navigating identity shifts, life transitions, emotional challenges, and questions about purpose, relevance, contribution, and legacy.

Who do you work with?

We primarily work with accomplished men and women aged 45 to 75 who feel stuck in an identity that no longer fits them, and want to rediscover who they are, recognise where they still matter, heal what is holding them back, and activate their purpose so they can confidently step into a meaningful next chapter.

Why do you focus on adults aged 45 to 75?

Because this stage of life often brings important questions. Children may have grown up. Careers may be changing. Retirement may be approaching. People often begin reassessing who they are and what matters most.

What makes Purpose2Destiny different from other coaching businesses?

Our approach is calm, reflective, compassionate, and deeply human. We do not use pressure based coaching, performance driven transformation tactics, or aggressive motivation strategies. Clarity comes before action. Healing happens gently. Purpose is discovered, not manufactured.

Who are Taiwo and Kehinde Adesina?

Twin sisters, qualified lawyers, certified Life Coaches, Business Coaches, NLP Practitioners, holders of a Diploma in Counselling, and founders of Purpose2Destiny. They combine professional expertise with lived experience to guide clients through meaningful personal transformation.

Coaching Questions

What is coaching?

Coaching is a collaborative process that helps people gain clarity, identify goals, overcome challenges, and move forward with confidence.

What does "clarity led coaching" mean?

It focuses on helping people understand themselves more deeply before making major life decisions — reflecting honestly, identifying what feels misaligned, and making decisions from calm awareness rather than fear or urgency.

How is coaching different from counselling?

Counselling often focuses on understanding and working through past experiences. Coaching focuses on helping people move forward from where they are now toward where they want to be.

How is coaching different from therapy?

Therapy is designed to diagnose and treat mental health conditions. Coaching focuses on personal growth, clarity, self-awareness, decision making, confidence, purpose, and future direction.

Do you work online?

Yes. We work with clients online, making our services accessible regardless of location.

How long are coaching sessions, and how many do I need?

Most sessions last between 45 and 60 minutes. Some clients benefit from one session; others choose longer support packages lasting several weeks or months. We help clients choose support that feels appropriate and sustainable.

Is coaching confidential?

Yes. Everything discussed during coaching sessions remains confidential, subject to legal and ethical obligations.

Emotional Wellbeing Questions

Is this therapy or counselling?

No. We are coaches and mentors, not therapists or medical professionals. If deeper mental health support is needed, we may encourage clients to seek appropriate therapeutic or clinical support alongside coaching.

What if I feel emotionally overwhelmed, or do not know what I want anymore?

Many clients arrive feeling emotionally tired, uncertain, or overwhelmed. You do not need to hide that here. Our approach is calm and supportive, and we move at a pace that feels manageable and emotionally safe.

Career & Reinvention Questions

Can you help with career reinvention?

Yes. We support clients considering career changes, exploring purpose-led work, transitioning toward retirement, starting businesses later in life, or rebuilding confidence after career shifts.

Is it too late for reinvention after 50 or 60?

Absolutely not. Many people discover deeper clarity, confidence, purpose, and fulfilment later in life. We believe: late beginnings are still right on time.

What if I want slower living instead of another ambitious career?

That is completely valid. Not every next chapter needs to be bigger, louder, or busier. We support clients seeking peace, simplicity, meaningful contribution, and steadier living too.

The Purpose2Destiny Journey

Do I need to complete all four programmes?

No. Each programme can be taken independently. However, many clients benefit from progressing through the full journey: Identity Reset, Relevance Reset, Emotional Renewal Reset, and Destiny Activation.

Which programme should I start with?

Many clients begin with Identity Reset. Others start with the area that feels most urgent.

Do I need to start a business to find my legacy?

No. Purpose and legacy can be expressed through family, mentoring, volunteering, community leadership, creative projects, faith-based service, relationships, teaching, coaching, professional expertise, or acts of kindness.

Pricing & Practical Questions

How much does coaching cost?

Pricing varies depending on the level of support, programme structure, and duration. We offer single sessions, standard coaching packages, and workshops and speaking engagements. See each coaching programme page for current pricing.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Flexible payment options may be available depending on the programme selected.

Is there any pressure to sign up?

No. The purpose of the conversation is clarity, not sales pressure. Many people spend time reflecting before deciding whether coaching is right for them.

What if I feel too old to change?

We hear this often. Our experience tells us otherwise. Growth does not have an age limit. Purpose does not expire. Contribution does not end at retirement.

Speaking & Partnerships

Do you offer speaking engagements?

Yes. Taiwo and Kehinde Adesina are available for conferences, workshops, corporate wellbeing events, leadership sessions, faith based events, community programmes, and podcasts and interviews. Topics include identity shifts, reinvention, emotional resilience, and meaningful living after 45.

Do you collaborate with organisations and professionals?

Yes. We welcome referral partnerships with coaches, therapists, HR professionals, retirement specialists, wellness practitioners, career consultants, faith organisations, and community groups.
